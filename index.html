Krishna Homeo Health Care

Dr Sobha Devi is a homoeopathy doctor with 30+ years of experience. Started her career in 1990 as an assistant doctor under the guidance of Dr Vara Prasad (MS General Surgeon) at Vanaja Ramachandra nursing home in Cherukupalli. Opened her first homeo clinic in 1992 at Tenali and offered services to the locals for 15 years. In 2005, served the Gynaecology & Obstetrics wing run by Dr Vedamani at Chaitanya Praja Vydyasala. Worked with one of the largest hospitals in Rajahmundry handling 20 deliveries daily & numerius OP cases covering surrounded village's public health issues. Since 2006, Sobha Devi is offering her experienced services at clinic "Krishna Homeo Health care" in Machavaram Down in Vijayawada.
